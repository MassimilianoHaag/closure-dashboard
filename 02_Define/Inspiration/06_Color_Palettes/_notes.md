# Color Palettes — Reference Notes

**What goes here:** Palette screenshots, color system references, dark mode treatments from professional tools. Collect from Coolors, Refactoring UI, actual app screenshots — whatever shows the palette in context.

**What we're trying to learn:**
- What's the difference between "warm neutral" and "beige"? Between "dark mode" and "cold grey"?
- How do professional tools use accent color sparingly — one moment of color, not a brand statement?
- What background/surface temperature creates intimacy without going dark?

**Look for:** Palettes with one considered accent on an otherwise neutral field. The accent should feel earned, not decorative. Avoid anything that reads as a brand color system — Closure is a personal tool, not a product family.

**What to bring back:** 3–5 palette samples with hex values noted. Tag each: warm / cool / neutral / dark.
