# Reflective Apps — Reference Notes

**What goes here:** Day One, Bear, Craft, personal journaling and note-taking tools with a private, reflective register. Screenshots, links, UI details.

**What we're trying to learn:**
- How do these apps make writing feel safe rather than performative?
- How do they handle the transition from raw capture to finished artifact?
- What typographic and spacing choices signal "this is yours" vs. "this is for an audience"?
- How do they treat the writing cursor / input area — is it inviting or clinical?

**Look for:** The moment before writing starts. The empty text field. Does it feel like a place you'd confide in, or a form you'd fill out?

**What to bring back:** 2–3 screenshots of input states that feel right for Closure's capture moment.
