# Voice-First Products — Reference Notes

**What goes here:** Otter.ai, voice memo apps, Whisper interfaces, Loom (recording state), any product where the core action is speaking.

**What we're trying to learn:**
- How do they visualize "recording in progress" without making it feel like a medical device?
- How do they communicate the transition from listening → processing → done?
- What does a microphone button look like when it's the primary action on a screen?
- How do waveform animations work — calming vs. anxious?

**Look for:** The active recording state specifically. Does it feel like the product is with you, or watching you?

**What to bring back:** The 1–2 recording UI treatments that feel most human. Note what they avoid (timers, aggressive waveforms, clinical red dots).
