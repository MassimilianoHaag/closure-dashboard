# Empty States — Reference Notes

**What goes here:** Screenshots of empty gallery states, empty inbox states, first-run experiences from tools with private collections. Day One's empty journal, Linear's empty project, iCloud Photo Library before any photos.

**What we're trying to learn:**
- How do good products make an empty gallery feel intentional rather than broken or unfinished?
- How does the empty state set expectations without being preachy or over-explaining?
- What copy tone works — directive ("Add your first capture"), inviting ("Your work lives here"), or silent?
- When is illustration appropriate vs. when does it undermine the register?

**Look for:** Empty states that make you want to fill them — not out of guilt, but because the space feels worth filling.

**What to bring back:** 2 examples (one that works, one that doesn't). This directly informs Closure's first-time user experience.
