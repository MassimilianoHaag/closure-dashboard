## Decision

Variant A is the Pass 1 direction. A bottom sheet in dark register rises from the lower ~45–50% of the mobile viewport, overlaying the completed Recording screen (Variant B — Piazzolla italic transcription + espresso stop dot) which is visible but dimmed behind the sheet. A thin horizontal rule in warm off-white at low opacity marks the sheet's top edge — no SaaS modal chrome, no X close button, no harsh card edges.

**Content hierarchy:**
1. Piazzolla italic headline: *"Want to add visuals while it's fresh?"* — the only Piazzolla element on the sheet
2. Muted Inter subtext: *"Screenshots, mockups, or anything that supports the story. You can always add them later."*
3. Cream `#EFEBE4` image icon + *"Add visuals"* Inter text link — primary action, readable by elimination
4. *"Skip for now"* — muted Inter, clearly subordinate, no friction

**No espresso accent on this screen.** Applied the "omit rather than force" interpretation used on the AI Reflection screen. The primary action is a text link + icon: neither element has sufficient mass to carry espresso against charcoal. Cream reads as primary by elimination.

**Emotional register:** Gentle offer, not a demanding modal. Mara just completed her ritual. This is a quiet invitation that appears once and is easy to dismiss. The ritual is complete; this is optional.

**Composite quality:** Figma Make rendered the overlay cleanly without manual intervention. The underlying Recording screen (Piazzolla italic transcription, espresso stop dot) is visible through the dimming layer in the final PNG.
